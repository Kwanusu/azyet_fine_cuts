Privacy Policy

At Azyet Fine Cuts, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you visit our shop or use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment through our site, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Service Preferences (e.g., Signature Cut, Beard Sculpting)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for business operations, specifically:

  • To confirm and manage your appointments.
  • To send automated reminders via email or SMS.
  • To notify you of any schedule changes or shop updates.

3. Data Security

Your personal information is stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or lease our client lists to third parties. Access to your data is limited to authorized staff who need the information to perform their grooming services.

4. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to social media platforms (Instagram, TikTok). Please note that we do not control the privacy policies of these external sites.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your data or wish to have your information removed from our booking system, please contact us at hello@azyetcuts.com.
