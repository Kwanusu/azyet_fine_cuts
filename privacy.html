Privacy Policy

At Azyet Fine Cuts, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal data you share with us. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you visit our shop or use our website.

1. Information We Collect

When you book an appointment through our site, we collect the following information:

  • Full Name
  • Phone Number
  • Email Address
  • Service Preferences (e.g., Signature Cut, Beard Sculpting)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your information strictly for business operations, specifically:

  • To confirm and manage your appointments.
  • To send automated reminders via email or SMS.
  • To notify you of any schedule changes or shop updates.

3. Data Security

Your personal information is stored securely. We do not sell, rent, or lease our client lists to third parties. Access to your data is limited to authorized staff who need the information to provide grooming services.

Terms of Service

By booking a service with Azyet Fine Cuts, you agree to the following terms and conditions:

1. Cancellation Policy

We value our barbers' time. Please provide at least 24 hours' notice if you need to cancel or reschedule. Repeated "no-shows" may require a non-refundable deposit for future bookings.

2. Arrival Time

Please arrive at least 5 minutes before your scheduled time. If you are more than 15 minutes late, we may need to reschedule your appointment to ensure our other clients are seen on time.

3. Right of Refusal

Our shop maintains a professional and respectful environment. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone demonstrating disrespectful or inappropriate behavior toward our staff or other clients.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding your data or these terms, please contact us at hello@azyetcuts.com.
