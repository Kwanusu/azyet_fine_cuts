Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 28, 2026

At Azyet Fine Cuts, we value your privacy. We only collect information that you voluntarily provide when booking an appointment (Name, Phone, Email).

1. Data Usage

Your data is used solely to manage bookings and send appointment reminders. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

2. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Terms of Service

1. Appointment Policy

Appointments can be cancelled or rescheduled up to 24 hours in advance. Late arrivals of more than 15 minutes may result in a modified service or rescheduling to ensure we remain on time for other clients.

2. Professional Conduct

We pride ourselves on being a community space. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone demonstrating disrespectful, aggressive, or inappropriate behavior toward our staff or other clients.

3. Pricing

Prices are subject to change. Any changes in pricing will be updated on our Services page and discussed prior to the commencement of your cut or shave.
